The 4 Layer Framework
Inside TWS, we teach a 4 Layer Framework for building your AI Supercycle Portfolio — each layer serving a specific purpose in creating a well-balanced, long-term portfolio centered around the TWS thesis.
What You’re Looking At
Three example long-term portfolios showing how the layers fit together visually: Core ETFs · Thematic ETFs · Core Stocks · Asymmetric Bets. Use the position count, allocations, and sector mix as a balanced starting framework.
Tickers Are Examples, Not Prescriptions
Every stock and ETF shown is one option from the TWS Stock Playbook. Swap any position for another name you prefer — regardless of asset class or sector. If you see AVGO as a Core Stock, you could use TSM, AMD, NVDA, or any name you have conviction in. The decision is always yours.
